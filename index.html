Contact your organization’s onboarding supervisor for instructions to connect to one of P1’s chat servers. Interested in becoming a customer? Learn more at https://p1.dso.mil.

General Air Force Mobile Apps

  • Aim High App
    A comprehensive recruiting and engagement tool. Features career exploration, fitness routines, recruiter connection, and following Basic Military Training (BMT).
  • USAF Connect
    Centralizes information for Airmen: directories, interactive maps, events, news, fitness calculators, and unit-specific sub-apps.
  • PTSD Coach
    Helps manage PTSD symptoms with tracking, stress tools, and support links.
  • My Military OneSource
    Resources for service members and families: child care, counseling, tax help, and educational resources.
  • Military News by Military.com
    News feed, pay and benefits updates, and military discounts.
  • American Forces Network (AFN) Apps
    Access AFN radio/TV with live streams, on-demand content, and regional stations.
  • C2IMERA
    Real-time base resource management for incident management and emergency coordination.
